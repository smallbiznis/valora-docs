title: Invoices
description: From rated usage to customer-facing billing statements.
---

Invoices aggregate ledger entries into a customer-facing document.

## Invoice lifecycle
- Draft: aggregating open ledger entries.
- Finalized: frozen for delivery and payment.
- Paid/closed: reconciled with payments and adjustments.

## Line items
Each line item references the ledger entries that produced it, making disputes and audits straightforward.
