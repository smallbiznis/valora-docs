---
title: Products & pricing
description: Define what you sell and how customers are billed.
---

Products describe what you offer. Prices describe how you charge for it.

## Product structure
- Products can include multiple pricing components.
- Components can be recurring, usage-based, or tiered.

## Pricing models
- Flat subscription (per-seat or per-organization).
- Usage-based (per request, per GB, per job).
- Hybrid (subscription + usage).

## Metadata
Pricing objects support metadata for downstream analytics and reporting.
